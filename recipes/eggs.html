Scrambled Eggs

Description

Fluffy not wet eggs straight from the pan

Ingredients

  • eggs...
  • salt

Steps

  1. Turn on a stove burner to oabout 3/4 of the maximum heat.
  2. Crack your eggs into a bowl.
  3. take a fork and whisk until there is a somewhat constistent color.
  4. pour eggs onto pan on the stove.
  5. Let it sit for a little until the bottom of the eggs gets more solid.
  6. Once it is a little solid, start breaking up the eggs
  7. After that, repeat the process of letting egg become solid and breaking up.
  8. Repeat until the desired size is met.
  9. Serve on plate. Add salt.
