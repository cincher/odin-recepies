Peanut Butter and Jelly

Description

The easiest thing to make in the kitchen

Ingredients

  • Peanut butter
  • Any jelly/jam of your liking
  • 2 slicing of bread

Steps

  1. Take your slices of bread and put them on a plate.
  2. Take a butter knife and spread the peanut butter on one slice of bread.
  3. Clean off the butter knife and spread the jelly/jam on the other slice of bread.
  4. Now put the two slices together so the two spreads touch.
